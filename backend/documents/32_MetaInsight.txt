What is Metabolic Therapy
Metabolic therapy is a ground breaking approach to cancer treatment that focuses on altering the body’s metabolism to inhibit cancer growth. By targeting the metabolic vulnerabilities of cancer cells, this therapy aims to starve cancer cells of their primary fuel sources —glucose and glutamine— while supporting the health and vitality of normal cells.

The Press/Pulse Protocol
How does it work?
The Press/Pulse protocol is a strategic combination of chronic and acute metabolic stressors designed to disrupt cancer cell metabolism. This innovative approach involves:

Press: Sustained metabolic stress through a calorie-restricted ketogenic diet, which reduces glucose availability and promotes ketone production.

Pulse: Intermittent acute stressors, such as hyperbaric oxygen therapy and metabolic drugs, to further weaken and eliminate cancer cells.
